## Current product shape

The intended product is primarily a Claude/ChatGPT-style learning Skill specified in Markdown. The Python package is a deterministic, testable rule-selection and safety-planning layer plus JSON CLI. During a real request, the Skill should detect topic, task, language, and explicit/distinctive lens intent; apply safety and correctness precedence; organize teaching through the selected lens; ground the real concept; give one small example; and finish with one focused check or next step. Only selection, safety classification, conflict handling, and structured-plan assembly are currently executable.
